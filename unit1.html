Unit 1: Hotel Communication

WH-Questions for real hotel situations

Practice What, Where, When, Why, Who, Which, How, How much, How many, How long and How far with hotel vocabulary.

Voltar ao Dashboard
Core vocabulary

Hotel words

room, reservation, receptionist, lobby, elevator, key card, breakfast, gym, swimming pool, restaurant, taxi, bus, airport, luggage, passport, ID, phone number, email address.

Section A — Information & Doubts

Listening, Reading and Writing

Complete or build these WH-questions in sequence:

  • What time is breakfast?
  • Where is the gym?
  • When does the restaurant close?
  • How much is the room?
Section B — Polite Requests & Transport

Speaking and Requests

Practice polite requests with transport and room needs.

  • How can I call a taxi?
  • Can I have another towel?
  • Could you help me?
Section C — Check-in & Reservations

Interactive simulation

Use: “I'd like to make a reservation”, ID, phone number, email address and “What is the Wi-Fi password?”.

Guest: I'd like to make a reservation.

Receptionist: Can I have your ID, phone number and email address?

Guest: What is the Wi-Fi password?
