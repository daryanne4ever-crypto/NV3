Activity 2: Polite Requests & Transport

Practice making polite requests and asking for transportation

Exercise 1: Complete the Polite Requests

How can I call a taxi?

Vocabulary: phone, reception, number, dial

Can I have another towel?

Vocabulary: towel, pillow, blanket, extra

Could you help me with my luggage?

Vocabulary: luggage, bag, suitcase, bellhop

How far is the bus station?

Vocabulary: bus, station, far, distance, minutes

Exercise 2: Transportation Scenarios

Scenario: You need to go to the airport

What would you ask the receptionist?

Use: "Could you...", "Can I...", "How long...", "How far..."
